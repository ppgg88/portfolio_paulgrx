Timetable software

Development of a timetable management software for a high-school.

EDT indiv is a tool that I developed to answer a problem my mother had. She has to plan individualized lessons for a large number of students. The software allows to fill in the timetables quickly, to distribute them to the students and the teacher concerned by email or via QR code and to allow the teacher to declare absences.
